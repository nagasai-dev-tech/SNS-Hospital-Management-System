about us

WELCOME TO SNS!

A state-of-the art modern facility in the heart of the ANDHRA PRADESH state, it is spread over 10 acres and has a built-up area of over 440000 square feet. Currently the hospital has 300+ beds with a capacity to expand to 400 beds.
SNS Hospital, Anantapur is a tertiary care flagship unit of the SNS Hospitals Group.The Hospital focuses on centres of excellence like Cardiac Sciences, Neuro Sciences, Orthopaedics including Knee Replacement, Hip Replacement and Spine Surgery, Cancer, Emergency Medicine and Solid Organ Transplants besides the complete range of more than 35 allied medical disciplines under the same roof.
SNS Hospital, Anantapur provides holistic healthcare that includes prevention, treatment, rehabilitation and health education for patients, their families and clients by touching their lives..

FAQ

+

What are the hospital's visiting hours?

Our general visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM. For ICU and critical care units, visiting hours are restricted to ensure patient safety. Please contact the department for specific timings.

+

How can I book an appointment with a doctor?

You can book an appointment by calling our help desk, visiting our website's "Book Appointment" section, or directly through our hospital's mobile app.

+

Does the hospital provide emergency services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency services with a fully equipped emergency department, advanced life-support ambulances, and on-call specialists.

+

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, and online payment methods. We also accept insurance from various providers. Please check with our billing desk for details.

+

Does the hospital accept health insurance?

Yes, we accept most major health insurance providers. Please provide your insurance details during registration to confirm coverage for the required services.

+

What facilities are available for patients' families?

We offer facilities such as a cafeteria, waiting lounges, parking areas, and family consultation rooms. For long-term stays, nearby accommodation options can be suggested.
